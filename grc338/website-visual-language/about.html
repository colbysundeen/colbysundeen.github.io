At The Original Greek, we invite you on a journey to experience the warmth and richness of Greek culture through our delectable cuisine. Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, our restaurant is a celebration of the vibrant flavors, heartfelt hospitality, and the time-honored traditions that define Greek dining.

From the azure waters of the Aegean to the sun-kissed hills of Crete, our menu is a culinary passport to Greece. Each dish is crafted with passion and authenticity, using the finest ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations. Whether you're savoring the flaky layers of our Spanakopita, indulging in the savory goodness of Moussaka, or diving into the freshness of a classic Greek Salad, every bite is a taste of the Mediterranean.

At the heart of our philosophy is a commitment to quality. We source the freshest local ingredients and imported Greek specialties to ensure an authentic and delightful dining experience.
